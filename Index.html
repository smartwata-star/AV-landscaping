Our Story

Started With a Shovel.
Built on Trust.

AV Landscape Design started in 2017 when founder Antonio Vargas bought a used truck, borrowed some equipment, and landed his first job redoing a front yard in Hemet. He'd spent years doing side landscaping work while working full-time at a nursery — and he always had one belief: do the job like it's your own house.

Word spread fast. Within two years we had a full crew and a waitlist. Today we serve homeowners across Hemet, Murrieta, Temecula, and the broader Inland Empire — specializing in artificial turf, custom hardscapes, and full backyard transformations that stand up to the SoCal heat.

We're still family-run. Antonio runs every project with the same mentality as day one — show up, do the work right, and make sure the client loves it before we leave.
